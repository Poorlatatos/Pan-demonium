**French-Style Roasted Chicken**

A Classic Recipe from the Heart of France

The art of French roasting transforms a simple chicken into a dish of golden perfection. Follow this traditional recipe to bring home the flavors of Parisian kitchens.

Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 90 mins | Serves: 4-6

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken (about 4-5 lbs)
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme
  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 1/2 cup white wine or chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven: Set to 425째F (220째C). Ensure the oven rack is in the middle position.
  2. Prepare the chicken: Pat the chicken dry with paper towels. Loosen the skin over the breasts with your fingers.
  3. Season and stuff: Rub butter under the skin. Mix garlic, thyme, rosemary, parsley, salt, and pepper, and rub it all over. Stuff the cavity with lemon wedges and onion.
  4. Truss and roast: Tie the legs together with kitchen twine. Place in a roasting pan, drizzle with olive oil, and pour white wine into the pan.
  5. Baste for flavor: Roast for 30 minutes, then baste every 15 minutes with pan juices.
  6. Final crisping: Increase the temperature to 450째F (230째C) for the last 15 minutes for crispy skin.
  7. Rest and serve: Remove from the oven, tent with foil, and let it rest for 10 minutes before carving.

Enjoy your authentic French-style roasted chicken with a side of buttered vegetables or fresh baguette!
