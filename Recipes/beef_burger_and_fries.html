**Beef Burger with Fries**

A Juicy Classic with Crispy Golden Fries

Experience the ultimate homemade burger—perfectly seasoned beef, melted cheese, and crisp vegetables, all nestled in a soft toasted bun. Served with crispy golden fries for a true diner-style experience.

Prep Time: 20 mins | Cook Time: 25 mins | Serves: 2

Ingredients:

Burger:

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 blend for juiciness)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese
  • 2 burger buns
  • 1 tbsp butter (for toasting buns)
  • 2 lettuce leaves
  • 2 tomato slices
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 4 slices pickles

Fries:

  • 2 large russet potatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable oil (for frying)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder

Instructions:

Making the Burger:

  1. Prepare the patties: In a bowl, mix the ground beef with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and Worcestershire sauce. Divide into 2 patties, slightly larger than the bun (as they shrink when cooked).
  2. Cook the patties: Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for 3-4 minutes per side for medium doneness. Add cheese on top during the last minute and cover to melt.
  3. Toast the buns: Spread butter on the inside of the buns and toast them in the skillet until golden brown.
  4. Assemble the burger: Spread mayonnaise on the bottom bun, then layer lettuce, tomato, the beef patty, pickles, ketchup, and mustard. Top with the toasted bun.

Making the Fries:

  1. Cut the potatoes: Wash, peel, and cut the potatoes into thin fries. Soak them in cold water for 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  2. Dry and season: Drain and pat them dry completely. Toss with a little salt and paprika.
  3. Fry the potatoes: Heat vegetable oil to 350°F (175°C) and fry in batches for 3-4 minutes until golden. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  4. Serve hot: Sprinkle with garlic powder and serve immediately with the burger.

Enjoy your classic homemade beef burger with crispy fries—perfect for a weekend treat or a backyard BBQ!
