**Margarita Pizza**

A Classic Italian Delight with Fresh Ingredients

Inspired by the traditional Neapolitan pizza, the Margarita pizza embodies simplicity and perfection—fresh tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, fragrant basil, and a crispy yet tender crust.

Prep Time: 20 mins | Rest Time: 60 mins | Cook Time: 10 mins | Serves: 2-4

Ingredients:

For the Dough:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the Sauce:

  • 1 can (14 oz) San Marzano tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp dried oregano

For the Toppings:

  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese (preferably buffalo mozzarella)
  • 6-8 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

Preparing the Dough:

  1. Activate the yeast: In a small bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Mix the dough: In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Add yeast mixture and olive oil, then mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead: On a floured surface, knead the dough for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Let it rise: Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover with a cloth, and let it rise for 60 minutes or until doubled in size.

Preparing the Sauce:

  1. Blend the tomatoes: In a bowl, crush the San Marzano tomatoes by hand or use a blender for a smoother consistency.
  2. Season: Stir in minced garlic, olive oil, salt, black pepper, and oregano.
  3. Let it rest: Allow the sauce to sit for at least 15 minutes to enhance the flavors.

Assembling and Baking:

  1. Preheat the oven: Set to 500°F (260°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  2. Shape the dough: Roll or stretch the dough into a 12-inch round on a floured surface.
  3. Add the sauce: Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce evenly, leaving a 1-inch border.
  4. Add toppings: Tear fresh mozzarella into small pieces and place evenly over the pizza.
  5. Bake: Transfer the pizza onto the hot pizza stone or baking sheet and bake for 7-10 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  6. Finish with basil: Remove from the oven and immediately top with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.

Slice and enjoy your homemade Margarita pizza—a true Italian classic bursting with fresh flavors!
